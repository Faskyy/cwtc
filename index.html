Hey everyone!

My name is Gabriella and I am the owner of CAN'T WEIGHT TO CLEAN! After graduating from Temple University in December of 2019, I had a hard time deciding on which one of my interests I wanted to pursue. The only thing I was certain of was that I wanted my own business. The irony of the situation was that my passion was in front of me, cleaning.

I started to clean my Mom's house when I was in middle school and it hasn't stopped since. Cleaning is a huge part of my life because I love to clean and it is a major de-stresser for me! I love putting my music on and zoning out while I magically transform a space with my extreme attention to detail. The before and afte transformation of a space can be very fulfilling. Since 2018, I've been cleaning gyms part time and realized I wanted to focus on cultivating a gym disinfecting business. Please come and support me on my journey!
